Research Projects

Revision Detection in Engineering Drawings

   (As part of my Master's thesis) 
  Mentor :  Dr. AN Rajagopalan
  Duration : July 2013 - July 2014
  • Proposed a new method to separate the textual and graphics part in engineering drawings
  • Developed a new algorithm to segment an engineering drawing into sub-drawings/views
  • Developed an algorithm to find differences in draft dimensions between two engineering drawings while accounting for variations in scale, position and orientation. On a test set of 86 images, algorithm finds differences with 94.8% with a false detection of 2.2%
